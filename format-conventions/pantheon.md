# Pantheon

For Pantheon events, each player should provide their own main deck, material deck, and boons for the event.

\
Main decks must be, at minimum, 60 cards with no more than one copy of any card.&#x20;

Material decks must have exactly 12 cards with at least one Lv 0 champion, and cannot contain more than one copy of any one card.

There is no sideboard for Pantheon.

In addition, there is a Pantheon Zone for which players must provide a Lesser and a Greater Boon to put into the Pantheon Zone at the start of the game.



\

\
Any cards with the same name as a card printed for the listed sets is legal for tournament play; the exact printing does not have to match those printed in the above products. Additionally, [Proxia’s Vault ](https://index.gatcg.com/cards?prefix=PRXY)provides cards legal for proxied tournament use. Pantheon has a ban list separate from other formats; please check Index for up to date card legality.
